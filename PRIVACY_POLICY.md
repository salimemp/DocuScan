# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DocScan Pro ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our document scanning and management application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

1. **Document Content**
   - Scanned document images
   - Extracted text from OCR processing
   - Document titles and tags
   - Comments and annotations

2. **User-Generated Content**
   - Electronic signatures you create
   - Document passwords (stored as secure hashes)
   - Custom folder names and organization preferences

3. **Communication Data**
   - Email addresses for signature requests
   - Recipient information when sharing documents

### Information Collected Automatically

1. **Device Information**
   - Device type and model
   - Operating system version
   - App version

2. **Usage Data**
   - Features accessed
   - Scan history
   - Export preferences

3. **Language Preferences**
   - Selected display language
   - RTL/LTR settings

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

1. **Document Processing**
   - Perform OCR text extraction using AI
   - Classify document types
   - Generate document summaries

2. **App Functionality**
   - Store and organize your documents
   - Enable password protection features
   - Facilitate document sharing

3. **Personalization**
   - Remember your language preference
   - Customize the app interface

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. **Google Gemini AI**
   - Used for document OCR and text extraction
   - Document images are processed to extract text
   - Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. **Resend (Email Service)**
   - Used for sending signature request notifications
   - Only processes email addresses for delivery
   - Resend's Privacy Policy applies: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. **MongoDB (Database)**
   - Stores document data and user preferences
   - Data is stored securely

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- Documents are stored in secure MongoDB databases
- Password-protected documents use bcrypt hashing
- Signatures are stored as encrypted image data
- Language preferences are stored locally using AsyncStorage

### Security Measures

- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Password hashes use industry-standard bcrypt algorithm
- Document images are compressed and securely transmitted
- No plaintext passwords are ever stored

## Your Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

1. **Access** - View all documents stored in your account
2. **Delete** - Remove any document permanently
3. **Export** - Download your documents in various formats
4. **Modify** - Edit document titles, tags, and content
5. **Secure** - Password-protect sensitive documents

### Data Deletion

To delete your data:
- Delete individual documents from the History screen
- Contact support for complete account deletion

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share documents with advertisers
- Use your documents for marketing purposes

We **MAY** share data:
- With your explicit consent when sharing documents
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or email.
